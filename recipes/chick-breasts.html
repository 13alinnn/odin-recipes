Air fryer chicken breasts

Who doesn't love chicken? We all love chicken breasts but cooking them in oil can be unhealthy for you!


You can make air fryer chicken breasts with the following ingredients
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
Cooking the actual dish is a 6 step process
  1. Preheat the air fryer at 190 celcius for 5 minutes
  2. Pat the chicken with paper towels and brush it with olive oil
  3. Season the chicken
  4. Cook it for 10 minutes on each side
  5. Rest it
  6. Serve
