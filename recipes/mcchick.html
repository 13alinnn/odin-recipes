Healthy McChicken

I've always loved fast food but ever since i starter being concerned about my health I've been avoiding it. This recipe is such a good replacement for McChickens I can't wait to share it!


Ingredient list
  • The patty
    • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (pounded to an even thickness or use pre-formed chicken patties)
    • 1 cup buttermilk
      • 1 cup flour
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
      • 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • For assembly
    • 2 hamburger buns
    • 2 tablespoons mayo
    • some lettuce
    • Optional: pickles
Instructions
  1. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk 30 minutes or up to overnight
  2. Preheat air fryer top 190 celcius for 5 minutes
  3. Prepare the breading
    • In a shallow dish, combine the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper
    • Remove the chicken from the buttermilk, letting the excess drip off, and dredge each piece in the flour mixture until fully coated. Shake off any excess flour
  4. Cook the chicken 10 minutes on both sides
  5. Assemble the burger
  6. Serve
