Healthy air fryer fries

Fries are the best side dish and everyone knows that. Let's learn how to cook them in the air fryer!

Ingredient list
  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: herbs
Instruction list
  1. Wash peel and cut the potatoes
  2. Soak the potatoes
  3. Season the fries
  4. Preheat the air fryer at 190 celcius for 5 minutes
  5. Cook the fries for 15-20 minutes
  6. Serve
